PROTOTYPING

Product desirability has become an important part of a consumer’s decision when buying any product. Kaber works with its partners to incorporate industrial design, advanced materials, and product engineering to create market leading products.
See how Kaber used Design to launch the Wilton BASH family of sledge hammers.

Industrial Design

At Kaber we believe the design process needs to be balanced with manufacturing realities. It is this understanding that allows Kaber to deliver products that achieve success in the market and not just pretty concepts.

RENDERING

Photorealistic rendering allows our customers to explore and communicate early concepts internally and externally to customers. Rendering is also used in marketing collateral as a replacement or in combination with product photography.

Graphic Design

Kaber offers comprehensive graphic design solutions including:
Product graphics
User interface
Technical manuals
Presentations

PROTOTYPING

Prototyping is an essential part of the product development process at Kaber. Materializing concepts at an early stage helps determine feasibility, identify potential challenges, and optimize the design before going to production.
